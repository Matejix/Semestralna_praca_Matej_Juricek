About Us

Welcome to Zoo zu

Our Zoo is a complex that is lovingly hand-built by us, the owners and at the same time the caregivers of animals. More than 100 animals live in an area exceeding one hectare, which is unique, especially in small stature. These are mainly miniatures of classic farm animals such as sheep, goats, horses, cows, llamas, donkeys, piglets, ducks, geese or chickens. The biggest hearts and the smallest enthusiasts of these mini-animals are, of course, children, so we dedicated the farm to them. However, even adult visitors will definitely not be bored with us.

And what sets us apart from other farms? We will allow you and your children unlimited access directly to the enclosure among the tame animals. You can look at the animals closely, feed them, pet them or observe how they behave in their natural environment.
